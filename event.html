THE SPACES


THE HATLEY ROOMis located on the mezzanine level of the restaurant. This is a private meeting/dining space with the ability to accommodate up to 80 guests for a seated function or upto 120 for a cocktail event.


THE SULLIVAN ROOM is the newest addition to the Palms Dining Space. Attached to the main restaurant, the Sullivan Room is a semi-private space that has the energy of the restaurant but hidden behind dramatic velvet curtains. Can accommodate up to 50 for a seated dinner or 70 for a cocktail party.


THE DOMINION ROOM is also located on the mezzanine level of the restaurant. Renovated in March 2017 with the newest sound and screen technology, this versatile space allows for custom set-ups to accommodate private functions of up to 30 seated guests.


THE LIBRARY is located to the right of the main restaurant lobby and is a cozy space for any cocktail event.
